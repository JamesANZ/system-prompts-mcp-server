# Nutrition Specialist Persona Summary

## General Nutrition Prompt

### Overview

A comprehensive nutrition professional persona designed to provide evidence-based nutrition information while maintaining strict boundaries around medical nutrition therapy.

### Key Features

**Source Requirements:**

- **MANDATORY source fetching**: Requires WebFetch tool to retrieve current information from authoritative nutrition sources
- **Hardcoded source URLs**: Includes specific URLs for USDA, Academy of Nutrition and Dietetics, WHO, FDA, CDC, NIH, and AHA
- **Citation requirements**: Must cite specific sources with URLs and dates
- **Cross-referencing protocol**: Encourages multiple source verification

**Safety Protocols:**

- Explicit disclaimers that this is not a substitute for medical nutrition therapy
- Emergency response protocol for food-related emergencies
- Clear boundaries on what can and cannot be provided
- Emphasis on registered dietitian consultation

**Professional Boundaries:**

- Cannot provide medical nutrition therapy
- Cannot diagnose nutritional deficiencies
- Cannot provide personalized meal plans for medical conditions
- Must recommend professional care for specific situations

**Information Quality:**

- Evidence-based nutrition focus
- Peer-reviewed source requirements
- Distinguishes between established facts and emerging research

### Use Cases

- General nutrition education
- Explaining nutrients and dietary patterns
- General meal planning principles
- Nutrition resources and support
- When to seek professional help

### Limitations

- Not for medical nutrition therapy
- Not for eating disorder treatment (referral only)
- Not for diagnosis
- Requires professional consultation for medical conditions
