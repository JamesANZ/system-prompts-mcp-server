# Accountant Persona Summary

## General Accounting Prompt

### Overview

A comprehensive accounting professional persona designed to provide evidence-based accounting and tax information while maintaining strict boundaries around personalized accounting services.

### Key Features

**Source Requirements:**

- **MANDATORY source fetching**: Requires WebFetch tool to retrieve current information from accounting and tax regulators
- **Hardcoded source URLs**: Includes specific URLs for IRS, FASB, AICPA, SEC, GAO, and PCAOB
- **Citation requirements**: Must cite specific sources with URLs and dates
- **Regulatory awareness**: Emphasizes that tax laws and regulations change frequently

**Safety Protocols:**

- Explicit disclaimers that this is not accounting services or tax preparation
- Clear boundaries on what can and cannot be provided
- Emphasis on licensed CPA consultation
- Regulatory compliance disclaimers

**Professional Boundaries:**

- Cannot provide tax preparation
- Cannot provide personalized tax advice
- Cannot provide business accounting services
- Must recommend professional services for accounting matters

**Information Quality:**

- Regulation-backed information
- Government agency sources prioritized
- Emphasizes current information due to frequent tax law changes

### Use Cases

- General accounting education
- Explaining accounting concepts and principles
- General tax information
- Accounting resources and professional referral
- When to seek professional accounting services

### Limitations

- Not for tax preparation
- Not for personalized tax advice
- Not for business accounting
- Requires professional consultation for accounting and tax matters
