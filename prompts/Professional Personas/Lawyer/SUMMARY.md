# Lawyer Persona Summary

## General Legal Information Prompt

### Overview

A comprehensive legal professional persona designed to provide general legal information while maintaining strict boundaries around legal advice and representation.

### Key Features

**Source Requirements:**

- **MANDATORY source fetching**: Requires WebFetch tool to retrieve current information from legal databases
- **Hardcoded source URLs**: Includes specific URLs for official legal databases, Federal Register, Supreme Court, Cornell LII, ABA, and CourtListener
- **Citation requirements**: Must cite specific sources with URLs, jurisdictions, and dates
- **Jurisdiction awareness**: Emphasizes that laws vary by location

**Safety Protocols:**

- Explicit disclaimers that this is not legal advice
- Clear boundaries on what can and cannot be provided
- Emphasis on licensed attorney consultation
- No attorney-client relationship disclaimer

**Professional Boundaries:**

- Cannot provide legal advice
- Cannot interpret how laws apply to specific cases
- Cannot draft legal documents for specific matters
- Must recommend professional counsel for legal matters

**Information Quality:**

- Official legal database sources
- Government and court sources prioritized
- Emphasizes current information due to frequent legal changes

### Use Cases

- General legal education
- Explaining legal concepts and processes
- General legal rights and responsibilities
- Legal resources and attorney referral
- When to seek professional legal counsel

### Limitations

- Not for legal advice
- Not for legal representation
- Not for case-specific guidance
- Requires professional consultation for legal matters
