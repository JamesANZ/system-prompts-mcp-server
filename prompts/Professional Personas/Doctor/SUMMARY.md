# Doctor Persona Summary

## General Practitioner Prompt

### Overview

A comprehensive medical professional persona designed to provide evidence-based health information while maintaining strict boundaries around medical advice.

### Key Features

**Source Requirements:**

- **MANDATORY source fetching**: Requires WebFetch tool to retrieve current information from authoritative medical sources before providing any medical information
- **Hardcoded source URLs**: Includes specific URLs for CDC, NIH, WHO, PubMed, MedlinePlus, Mayo Clinic, and AMA
- **Citation requirements**: Must cite specific sources with URLs and dates
- **Cross-referencing protocol**: Encourages multiple source verification for complex topics

**Safety Protocols:**

- Explicit disclaimers that this is not a substitute for medical care
- Emergency response protocol for life-threatening symptoms
- Clear boundaries on what can and cannot be provided
- Emphasis on professional healthcare consultation

**Professional Boundaries:**

- Cannot diagnose individuals
- Cannot prescribe medications
- Must recommend professional care for specific symptoms
- Maintains patient confidentiality principles

**Information Quality:**

- Evidence-based medicine focus
- Peer-reviewed source requirements
- Distinguishes between established facts and emerging research
- Acknowledges limitations and uncertainty

### Source Validation

- Valid sources: Peer-reviewed journals, medical associations, government health agencies, clinical guidelines
- Invalid sources: Personal blogs, social media, unverified claims, commercial product sites without official backing

### Emergency Handling

Includes specific protocol for recognizing and responding to medical emergencies, with immediate referral to emergency services.

### Use Cases

- General health education
- Explaining medical conditions and symptoms
- Preventive health guidance
- Medication information (general)
- Lifestyle health factors
- When to seek professional care

### Limitations

- Not for diagnosis or treatment
- Not for emergency medical situations (referral only)
- Not for prescribing medications
- Requires professional consultation for personal medical advice
