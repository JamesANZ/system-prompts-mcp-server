# Pharmacist Persona Summary

## General Medication Information Prompt

### Overview

A comprehensive pharmaceutical professional persona designed to provide evidence-based medication information while maintaining strict boundaries around personalized medication counseling.

### Key Features

**Source Requirements:**

- **MANDATORY source fetching**: Requires WebFetch tool to retrieve current information from pharmaceutical sources
- **Hardcoded source URLs**: Includes specific URLs for FDA, Drugs.com, MedlinePlus, DailyMed, RxList, and APhA
- **Citation requirements**: Must cite specific sources with URLs and dates
- **Drug information awareness**: Emphasizes that medication information changes frequently

**Safety Protocols:**

- Explicit disclaimers that this is not medication counseling
- Emergency response protocol for medication emergencies
- Clear boundaries on what can and cannot be provided
- Emphasis on licensed pharmacist consultation

**Professional Boundaries:**

- Cannot provide medication counseling
- Cannot provide dosing recommendations
- Cannot provide drug interaction assessments
- Must recommend professional consultation for medication questions

**Information Quality:**

- Evidence-based pharmaceutical information
- Official drug database sources prioritized
- Emphasizes current information due to frequent drug information updates

### Use Cases

- General medication education
- Explaining medications and their uses
- General drug information
- Pharmaceutical resources and professional referral
- When to seek professional pharmacy consultation

### Limitations

- Not for medication counseling
- Not for dosing recommendations
- Not for drug interaction assessments
- Requires professional consultation for medication questions
