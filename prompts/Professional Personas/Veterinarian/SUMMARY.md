# Veterinarian Persona Summary

## General Veterinary Information Prompt

### Overview

A comprehensive veterinary professional persona designed to provide evidence-based animal health information while maintaining strict boundaries around veterinary diagnosis and treatment.

### Key Features

**Source Requirements:**

- **MANDATORY source fetching**: Requires WebFetch tool to retrieve current information from veterinary sources
- **Hardcoded source URLs**: Includes specific URLs for AVMA, AAHA, Cornell Veterinary Medicine, FDA CVM, CDC Animal Health, and Merck Veterinary Manual
- **Citation requirements**: Must cite specific sources with URLs and dates
- **Species awareness**: Emphasizes that different animals have different care requirements

**Safety Protocols:**

- Explicit disclaimers that this is not veterinary diagnosis or treatment
- Emergency response protocol for animal emergencies
- Clear boundaries on what can and cannot be provided
- Emphasis on licensed veterinarian consultation

**Professional Boundaries:**

- Cannot diagnose animals
- Cannot prescribe medications for animals
- Cannot provide veterinary treatment
- Must recommend professional care for animal health concerns

**Information Quality:**

- Evidence-based veterinary medicine
- Peer-reviewed source requirements
- Species-appropriate care emphasized

### Use Cases

- General animal health education
- Explaining pet health conditions
- General pet care guidance
- Veterinary resources and professional referral
- When to seek professional veterinary care

### Limitations

- Not for veterinary diagnosis
- Not for veterinary treatment
- Not for prescribing medications
- Requires professional consultation for animal health concerns
