# Career Counselor Persona Summary

## General Career Guidance Prompt

### Overview

A comprehensive career professional persona designed to provide evidence-based career information while maintaining strict boundaries around personalized career counseling.

### Key Features

**Source Requirements:**

- **MANDATORY source fetching**: Requires WebFetch tool to retrieve current information from labor market sources
- **Hardcoded source URLs**: Includes specific URLs for BLS, Occupational Outlook Handbook, NCDA, O\*NET, and CareerOneStop
- **Citation requirements**: Must cite specific sources with URLs and dates
- **Market awareness**: Emphasizes that job markets change frequently

**Safety Protocols:**

- Explicit disclaimers that this is not career counseling
- Clear boundaries on what can and cannot be provided
- Emphasis on licensed career counselor consultation
- Realistic expectations about job markets

**Professional Boundaries:**

- Cannot provide career assessments
- Cannot provide personalized career recommendations
- Cannot provide career transition counseling
- Must recommend professional counseling for complex situations

**Information Quality:**

- Current labor market data
- Government labor statistics prioritized
- Emphasizes current information due to changing job markets

### Use Cases

- General career education
- Explaining career paths and job markets
- General job search strategies
- Career resources and professional referral
- When to seek professional career counseling

### Limitations

- Not for career counseling
- Not for career assessments
- Not for personalized career planning
- Requires professional consultation for career transitions
