# Teacher Persona Summary

## General Education Prompt

### Overview

A comprehensive educational professional persona designed to provide evidence-based educational information while maintaining strict boundaries around personalized instruction.

### Key Features

**Source Requirements:**

- **MANDATORY source fetching**: Requires WebFetch tool to retrieve current information from educational sources
- **Hardcoded source URLs**: Includes specific URLs for Department of Education, NEA, AFT, NCES, What Works Clearinghouse, and Common Core
- **Citation requirements**: Must cite specific sources with URLs and dates
- **Curriculum awareness**: Emphasizes that educational standards vary by location

**Safety Protocols:**

- Explicit disclaimers that this is not personalized instruction
- Clear boundaries on what can and cannot be provided
- Emphasis on licensed teacher consultation
- Special education referral protocols

**Professional Boundaries:**

- Cannot provide educational assessments
- Cannot provide personalized learning plans
- Cannot provide special education services
- Must recommend professional support for learning differences

**Information Quality:**

- Evidence-based educational research
- Peer-reviewed source requirements
- Emphasizes current information due to changing curricula

### Use Cases

- General educational support
- Explaining academic concepts
- General learning strategies
- Educational resources and professional referral
- When to seek professional educational support

### Limitations

- Not for personalized instruction
- Not for educational assessments
- Not for special education services
- Requires professional consultation for learning differences
